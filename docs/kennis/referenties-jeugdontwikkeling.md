# Referenties Jeugdontwikkeling

Bronverwijzingen uit het onderzoeks- en ontwerpproces van het vaardigheidsraamwerk v3.0. Deze documenten zijn verwijderd uit de repo; de kennis is verwerkt in het raamwerk en de app. Git history bevat de originele documenten.

## Wetenschappelijke bronnen

### Self-Determination Theory & Motivatie
- Deci, E.L. & Ryan, R.M. (1985). *Intrinsic Motivation and Self-Determination in Human Behavior.* Plenum Press.
- Deci, E.L. & Ryan, R.M. (2000). The "What" and "Why" of Goal Pursuits. *Psychological Inquiry*, 11(4), 227-268.
- Ryan, R.M. & Deci, E.L. (2017). *Self-Determination Theory.* Guilford Press.
- Vallerand, R.J. (2007). Intrinsic and Extrinsic Motivation in Sport and Physical Activity. In *Handbook of Sport Psychology* (3rd ed.).

### Plezier & Sport Enjoyment
- Scanlan, T.K. & Simons, J.P. (1992). The construct of sport enjoyment. In *Motivation in Sport and Exercise*.
- Scanlan, T.K. et al. (1993). An introduction to the Sport Commitment Model. *JSEP*, 15(1), 1-15.
- Visek, A.J. et al. (2015). The Fun Integration Theory. *JPAH*, 12(3), 424-433.
- Wiersma, L.D. (2001). Sources of enjoyment in youth sport questionnaire. *MPEES*, 5(3), 153-177.

### Dropout & Retentie
- Crane, J. & Temple, V. (2015). Dropout from organized sport among children and youth. *European Physical Education Review*, 21(1), 114-131.

### Mindset & Mentale Hardheid
- Dweck, C.S. (2006). *Mindset: The New Psychology of Success.* Random House.
- Duckworth, A.L. et al. (2007). Grit: Perseverance and Passion for Long-Term Goals. *JPSP*, 92(6), 1087-1101.
- Clough, P. et al. (2002). Mental toughness: The concept and its measurement. In *Solutions in Sport Psychology*.
- Gucciardi, D.F. et al. (2009). Advancing mental toughness research. *IRSEP*, 2(1), 54-72.

### Motorische Ontwikkeling
- Wormhoudt et al. (2018). *The Athletic Skills Model.* Routledge. + athleticskillsmodel.nl
- Balyi & Hamilton — LTAD Framework.
- Ulrich, D.A. (2019). *TGMD-3: Test of Gross Motor Development* (3rd ed.). Pro-Ed.
- Gallahue & Ozmun (2012). Motorische ontwikkeling, "golden age".

### Emotieregulatie & Ontwikkelingspsychologie
- Csikszentmihalyi, M. (1990). *Flow: The Psychology of Optimal Experience.* Harper & Row.
- Gross, J.J. (2015). Emotion Regulation: Current Status and Future Prospects. *Psychological Inquiry*, 26(1), 1-26.
- Piaget (1954). Preoperationeel en concreet-operationeel denken.
- Erikson (1968). Identiteitsontwikkeling.

### Talentidentificatie
- Beldman et al. (2026). Essential Characteristics for Talent Identification in Volleyball. *IJSSC*.
- Johnston, K. et al. (2018). Are youth sport talent identification systems necessary? *Sports Medicine - Open*, 4(18).
- Eady, J. (1993). *Practical Sports Development.* Longman.

### Scouting & Assessment
- Frontiers in Sports (2020). Pilot Study on the Reliability of the Coach's Eye.
- Journal of Sports Sciences (2023). Disagreement between talent scouts.
- PubMed (2024). Hull Soccer Behavioural Scoring Tool.
